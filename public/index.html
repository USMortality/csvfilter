xls2csv

Usage

xls2csv.com/data.csv?sheet=<Sheet1>&url=<url>

Examples:

Original XLS: https://file-examples-com.github.io/uploads/2017/02/file_example_XLS_10.xls

Link: https://xls2csv.com/data.csv?sheet=Sheet1&url=https://file-examples-com.github.io/uploads/2017/02/file_example_XLS_10.xls

Wget: wget "https://xls2csv.com/data.csv?sheet=Sheet1&rowsToSkip=0&url=https://file-examples-com.github.io/uploads/2017/02/file_example_XLS_10.xls"

Google Sheets: =IMPORTDATA("https://xls2csv.com/data.csv?sheet=Sheet1&rowsToSkip=0&url=https://file-examples-com.github.io/uploads/2017/02/file_example_XLS_10.xls")
